# FreelanceHub Branding

- Portal name: `FreelanceHub`
- Database file: `database/freelancehub_db.sql`
- Logo asset: `frontend/public/freelancehub-logo.svg`

The logo keeps the same premium black, orange, and white visual language as the original design, but shifts the mark toward freelance work:

- `F` monogram for FreelanceHub
- orange upward tool/arrow accent for momentum and opportunity
- white plus node suggesting connection, collaboration, and trusted intros
